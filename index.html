Alfie's Story

My Home

So I live in the ocean with my dolphin family which is called a pod. But i'm not the only animal that calls the oceans home. According to scientists there are over 1million species living in the ocean, that’s a lot of neighbors! My friends the blue whales or the octopus would be great examples. As well as in the ocean there are also 300 of those noisy neighbors flying above, the birds! Seagulls and pelicans would be examples of some of the more famous birds that depend on the ocean.

What's Going Wrong?

We all depend on our home, the ocean, to keep us happy, healthy and safe. But our home is a finally balanced ecosystem and everyday items for humans like plastic bottles and packaging upsets this balance in our home! Remember what’s good for humans isnt always good for my friends and I in the ocean! According to the Natural History Museum anywhere from 4.8 billion to 12.7 billion tonnes of plastic goes into the sea every year and we hate it!!

Friends of the sea

All this plastic and pollution is destroying our home and making my friends and I sick. Sadly over 1 million animals die each year in the ocean due to this pollution. But lucky for my pod and all the other animals who call the ocean home, some people are taking action and helping us clean up our home!
